Welcome to Haven, a service made by Adarsh, Aryan, and Sukrith to find a middleground between being social and
maintaining privacy. We have a variety of services based off of what you want to do. Please take a look at our
current options.

 - AI Chatbot
   - This has 'Raven' our "AI" and it responds to specific prompts

 - Anonymous Messaging
   - This is for messaging, anonymously

 - Anonymous Social Media
   - This is for social media posts that are anonymous

 - Private Video-Chatting
   - Currently unavailable, but it's supposed to host video calls without usernames (but faces can be shown if video is turned on)
